The next great paradigm shift in compute is coming.

we call it

First Person Compute

Just like the shift to mobile, it will affect everything. And just like mobile, exactly how it will affect everything is impossible to predict.

We know this paradigm shift is coming. We dont know where it is going. But we need to be ready.

So we need a different approach.

To embrace uncertainty, to experiment fast, to learn as we go, Hand-in-hand with the right partners.

We cannot do it alone.

To build not a north star, not a solution, but a movement.

A movement to build an ecosystem delivering the best this paradigm shift has to offer.

That takes 5 points.

Ignite

Creating the organizational gravity pull we need.

Our role is to see whats just over the horizon first and understand the potential risks and opportunities. To explore a new system that is in the making that will heavily impact our lives. And to be excited as designers that we can play a role in shaping that system and making peoples lives better.
We know that change is coming, but first we must build collective belief in and the need to react to it making others acknowledge and experience the coming paradigm change.
By building momentum, belief and conviction in key stakeholders we can make them put it on the agenda, to care about it and become promoters that enable exploration.

2023 plan

Empathize

Exploring the human experience of immersive possibilities.

Now that the topic is on the agenda and belief is building, we want to truly understand it.
Just like its impossible to fully architect a solution in such an emergent space, its impossible to size the business opportunity. Instead we want to understand the system, the mechanisms at play, and how this will impact peoples lives, how it will change perceptions, values, attitudes and behaviors both positively and negatively.
As designers and makers we know this system is being designed and therefor can be molded by us. We want to understand the human impact so we get a feel for which experiences really matter in peoples lives.

2023 plan

Kindle

Starting a fire in a good way

With deep insights into the paradigm shift and how it will affect peoples lives we get a feeling for opportunities to influence the system. Now we must engage others around the opportunity, to join us in exploring & defining experiences and possibilities.
The goal is not to design a complete solution, instead to create examples of experiences that matter to us as provocations and inspiration. The CES work is a perfect example. We think Presence is an important value that can be explored as part of this new compute paradigm and designed a demo that shows how presence becomes part of project based work.

2023 plan

Experiment

Start, build, learn, repeat

Instead of waiting for a fully baked solution, we start today building experiments with partners that we can try with customers. We learn by doing & observing, exploring new angles and ideas as they emerge.
We build an alliance of partners, fellow believers, that allow us to create experiments that can be put into context for learning. Find out what happens when we put solutions out into the real world. Design experiments that are open to interpretation and observe what people actually do to inform next steps

2023 plan

Anchor

Making it real

We ground everything we do by creating a platform that is owned by an alliance of partners. All the investments made in earlier phases flow into the platform. This is where we share IP, define standards, document and expose solutions, share SDKs and APIs, work on a common architecture and more.
